DIRECT REGION LOCK EXPERIMENTAL force a region, no VPN
Blocks the match-server IP ranges of every other region in Windows Firewall, so FragPunk can only place you in the region you pick — and you connect directly (no VPN hop, often lower ping than a VPN to the same region). Your lobby, login and voice always stay open (whitelisted by port). Auto-removes when you close Fragnetic. Improves as you play — each match teaches it more server ranges.

    Optional · VPN routing
    Get a free WireGuard config
    You only need this to improve ping to a far region. For your normal region, ping and region-lock already work with no VPN. Any provider's WireGuard .conf works — here's a free route:
    1. Create a free ProtonVPN account at protonvpn.com (the free tier includes WireGuard; server choice is limited on free).
    2. In your Proton account, open Downloads → WireGuard configuration, pick a platform (Windows) and a server, and download the .conf file.
    3. Back here, hit Import .conf… and select the file(s). Name them with a region code if you like (e.g. US-NY.conf, DE.conf) — the app maps them to regions automatically.
    4. The region cards light up with a Connect button. That's it.
    Prefer another provider (Mullvad, IVPN, etc.)? Their WireGuard .conf files import the same way. Fragnetic never sees your VPN credentials — the file stays on your PC.
