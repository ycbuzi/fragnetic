## Fragnetic 20.21 — first-run "Getting Started" checklist

New users told us the app is powerful but it wasn't obvious *what to do first*. There was a
guided tour of where things live, but nothing that walked you through actually setting up.

### New — Getting Started
On first launch (right after you sign in) Fragnetic now shows a short **Getting Started**
checklist that walks you through the real setup steps, each with live status pulled from your
own machine:

1. **Check your PC is ready** — reads your System Check (GPU → recommended model, WebView2,
   recording, disk…) and tells you if you're good to go. → jumps to the details.
2. **Get your AI models** — shows how many GB of the models that fit *your* GPU still need
   downloading (or a ✓ if they're already there). → takes you to the downloads.
3. **Region routing (optional)** — explains that ping + region-lock work with no VPN, and how
   to drop in a WireGuard `.conf` if you want one.
4. **Learn where everything lives** — launches the 30-second guided tour.

You can skip any step and reopen the whole thing anytime with the new **🚀 Getting started**
button on the **Setup** tab. Existing users aren't re-onboarded. After you're set up it hands
off to the friendly "make it yours" wallpaper picker.

Update by downloading Fragnetic-Setup.zip below and replacing your existing folder.
