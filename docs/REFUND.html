Fragnetic — Refund Policy

*Draft — last updated 2026-07-02. Align the numbers below with whatever your

payment processor requires (Gumroad/Lemon Squeezy/Stripe all have their own

minimum refund-window expectations for card-network compliance).*

14-day money-back guarantee

If Fragnetic doesn't work for you, request a refund within 14 days of

purchase and we'll refund it, no questions asked, as long as:

  • The request is for a first-time purchase (not a renewal), and
  • You haven't already received a refund for a previous Fragnetic purchase.

How to request one

Email *[support address]* with your order ID (from your purchase receipt) and

"refund" in the subject line. Refunds are processed back to your original

payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank/processor.

Subscriptions

If you're on a subscription tier, canceling stops future billing immediately;

you keep access through the end of the period you already paid for. The

14-day guarantee above applies to your first billing cycle only.

What isn't covered

  • Game account actions taken by FragPunk's operator (bans, suspensions) are

outside our control and not a basis for a refund of Fragnetic itself — see

the EULA's disclaimer.

  • Hardware incompatibility that was disclosed in the system requirements

before purchase.

Try before you buy

Fragnetic includes a 14-day free trial of Pro-tier features before any

payment is required — please use it. It's the best way to confirm

compatibility with your system and workflow before buying.
